Vegetarian lasagna

Description

This vegetarian lasagna is a delicious and hearty dish that’s perfect for family dinners or meal prep. It’s packed with layers of roasted vegetables, creamy béchamel sauce, and rich tomato sauce, all topped with melted cheese for the perfect golden-brown finish.

Whether you’re a seasoned vegetarian or just looking to cut back on meat, this lasagna is a satisfying and flavorful option that even meat-lovers will enjoy. Plus, it’s easy to make and great for leftovers!

Ingredients for 8 servings

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 200g (7oz) mushrooms, sliced
  • 800g (28oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250g (9oz) lasagna noodles
  • 500g (18oz) ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
  • 250g (9oz) grated mozzarella
  • 50g (2oz) grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce:

    Heat olive oil in a large pan. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent. Add zucchini, bell pepper, and mushrooms, cooking for about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15 minutes.

  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F)

  3. Assemble the lasagna:

    In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the sauce. Add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta, sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and cheese on top. Sprinkle Parmesan over everything.

  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  5. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
